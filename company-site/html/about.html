About Us

We Provide Clean Energy Solutions

The need for better sources of energy has increasingly risen over the past few years. With climate change and increasing population, clean energy resources have proven to be more prevalent.

Yana aims to be as collaborative as possible in providing the best and safest means of energy resources, that power meaningful and impactful areas in the economy.

Engaging the world to become a safer and more accomodating place for energy starts here. We are building a new world, and it starts with sustainable energy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect the efficient and innovative technologies to provide energy solutions to everyday energy problems.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create an energy conglomerate that provides jobs and the tools needed to build up Nigerian manufacturing infrastructure.
